Course Location

The in-person course will be hosted at the University of Costa Rica (UCR) Rodrigo Facio campus in San José, Costa Rica. The hands-on laboratory training sessions will be held in the Centro de Investigación en Ciencias del Mar y Limnología (CIMAR) molecular laboratories.
